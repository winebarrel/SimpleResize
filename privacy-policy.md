# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: March 21, 2026**

## Overview

SimpleResize ("the App") is developed by Genki Sugawara. This Privacy Policy explains how the App handles your information.

## Data Collection

SimpleResize does not collect, store, transmit, or share any personal data or usage information. The App operates entirely on your device.

## Photos and Camera

The App accesses your Photo Library and Camera solely to allow you to select or capture images for resizing. All image processing is performed locally on your device. No images or related data are uploaded to any server or shared with any third party.

## Analytics and Tracking

The App does not use any analytics tools, tracking technologies, or advertising frameworks.

## Third-Party Services

The App does not integrate with any third-party services that collect data.

## Children's Privacy

The App does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children under the age of 13.

## Changes to This Policy

If this Privacy Policy is updated, the changes will be reflected on this page with an updated date.

## Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sugawara@winebarrel.jp.
